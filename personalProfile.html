Personal Profile

I had a lot of fun with this. I have a strong interest in human psychology, particulary my own. I decided to spend a bit of money on a detailed Myers-Briggs test as I have wanted to do this for some time and I got some fantastic insights from it. Let me share a few with you below. To begin with...

Myer-Briggs Test

ENFP is considered a "people-centered creator". I would agree with that as a short analysis. I love to create and I do certainly take into account with those around me. Fortunately this report goes into great detail and there are some really interesting things that popped out to me from it. For a start I certainly wouldn't consider myself completely extroverted and this report agreed.

Some interesting statistics about my own personality I found from doing this test

As I said I don't believe I am 100% extraverted. I certainly enjoy a certain amount of time to myself. This was an interesting statistic

I also found this worth noting. I definitely prefer to work in a creative way and rely on my imagination to come up with the ideas that I implement into the software I create at work.

The report also came with this in depth analysis of each element that it tested for. This was a really interesting task and worth spending a bit of money for a full report.


Learning Style Test

My learning style test was from training.com.au. It showed that I am a visual learner which I definitely agree with. I like to see charts and graphs to make decisions and can extrapolate the information I need from these mediums easily Once I see how something is done I can apply it.

Creativity Test

My creativity score was from testmycreativity.com I found that I was pretty close to typical on this front which was a bit surprising however I was leaning towards curiosity. I like to try to stay away from the way things are always done and come up with new ways to do things which I think shows a level of curiosity.

What does this mean to me?

Taking all these results into account this means a number of things. I believe that my curiosity can be made a strong suit however it has its down falls and I need to be aware of this. I need to make sure that I don't overlook a solution to a problem specifically to do it in a different way. I think that I need to make sure that I don't let procrastination get the better of me while learning and try to stick to a schedule as best as I can as these can be seen as downfalls of my personality type. While I have known for a while that visual learning is the best way for me to retain information I will keep this in mind and make sure to create visual stimulus whenever possible during work and study.

How will this affect group work?

I believe my personality will allow me to work well in groups. I like to initiate discussions and open the floor to conversation about new ideas so I will keep this in mind while doing group work. Understanding how people work together and recognising others emotions will be key in keeping the peace in a group. Creating personal relationships with all the people in my team will be something that I ensure to do with this knowledge in mind. Encouraging creative thinking and out of the box ideas is a strength and I will play into this. I need to make sure that I am open to opposing ideas and criticism to make sure that I can put my best work forward and work with others in the team effectively.

Taking this into account while forming a team

Ensuring a diverse group of individuals in a group is important especially when it comes to personality types however this knowledge can also help to make sure there won't be personality clashes within a group. I believe I would look for other somewhat extraverted people to work with that will be open to communication within the group. I think getting a group member that is on the other end of the personality spectrum would help with keeping organised and maintaining order and schedule such as an ISTJ or ESTJ. I will certainly keep this information in mind at work and during my time studying to make sure my whole team gets the most out of our group work together.
